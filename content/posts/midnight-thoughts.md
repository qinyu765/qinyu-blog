# Wandering the Dark Hour

Sometimes, coding at 3 AM feels like entering a hidden hour between days. The world is quiet, the notifications stop, and it's just you and the logic.

*   Silence is golden.
*   Focus is finite.
*   Coffee is essential.

## Finding Balance

It is crucial to disconnect. The digital world is infinite, but our cognitive energy is not.
